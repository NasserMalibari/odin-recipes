Lasagne

Description

Lasagne is a combination of Lasagne sheets, sheets and bolognese.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Butter
  • Canned Tomato
  • Flour
  • Cheese
  • etc...

Instructions

  1. Smear meat on the base
  2. Lasagna Sheets
  3. Top with meat sauce and then some white sauce
  4. Reoeat again, lasagna sheets, meat sauce and then white sauce
  5. Topping - Cover with lasagna sheets, pour remaining white sauce then sprinkle with Cheese
